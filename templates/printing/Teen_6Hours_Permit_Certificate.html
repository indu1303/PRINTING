Congratulations on completing the first 6 hours of your Texas Parent-Taught Driver's Ed course. Attached please find your 6-Hour Permit Qualification Certificate (DE-964E). Now you are eligible to take the DPS permit test!

The state of Texas has certain requirements in order for you to obtain your instruction permit. We have outlined all of the requirements for you here:

You can print a PDF version of these instructions to refer back to at your convenience

1. Go to your local DPS office to take your Texas Permit Exam and vision test, and receive your Learner's Permit!

  • Bring the following to the DPS office to take your written permit exam:
  • Your 6-Hour Permit Qualification Certificate (DE-964E)
  • Proof of identity (such as a birth certificate)
  • Social Security card
  • Completed forms from the Parent Taught Driver Education Packet:
  • Application for TX driver's license (DL-14A)
  • Parental Driver Education Affidavit (DL-90A)
  • Verification of Enrollment and Attendance (VOE) form
  • Classroom Instruction Record (DL-91A) for PTDE
  • Proof of vehicle registration and insurance
  • $16 permit fee
  
 

2. Pass your written Texas Permit Exam and vision test

  • You have three opportunities within 90 days to successfully pass all tests

3. Continue with your Parent-Taught Driver's Ed course, and begin your behind-the-wheel training!

  • You must continue with your I DRIVE SAFELY course for your permit to remain valid
  • Fill out the training logs provided in the DPS Parent-Taught Education Packet as behind-the-wheel training is completed
  • The logs document the amount of time you spend in training
  • NOTE: 34 hours of documented behind-the-wheel training must be completed; 10 of those hours must be at night

Note: At the time of application for the instruction permit, the department will check the driving record of your parent or guardian. If he/she is found to be ineligible to teach our course due to a disqualifying conviction, or for failing to have a valid Texas driver license, you will be denied an instruction permit and all of your prior instruction time is invalid.
