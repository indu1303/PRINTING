What do I do with my certificate?
Both the student copy and the court copy of your completion certificate will be sent to you as a single document.

Student Copy - Please detach the student copy and keep it for your records.

Court Copy - detach the court copy and either mail or hand deliver it to the court. It is your responsibility to provide the court with a copy of your completion certificate by your due date.

Tracking your certificate progress
After you have given your completion certificate to the court. You can check to see if they have processed your completion by going to: https://www6.hsmv.state.fl.us/dlcheck/studentSearchPage. When you get to the DHSMV website, enter your driver's license number and your citation number (located on your citation).
